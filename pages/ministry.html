Ministries

Pathways has an array of ministries built to provide for our congregation, visitors, and neighbors. Each ministry plays a vital role in our church and has the ultimate purpose of making disciples and glorifying God.

Welcome & Care

Director: Younhe Shin

This ministry has the goal of welcoming newcomers with the love of Christ and connecting them with a small group. This ministry additionally gathers and shares prayer topics of members to help meet needs.

Worship

Director: Soojin Ko

This committee has the responsibility of leading and maintaining times of worship. This includes musical praise, audio and video operators, live streams, and more. Any worship requests should be passed through this committee.

Finance

Director: Greg Feigh

This committee has the responsibility of monitoring and keeping track of Church expenses and cash flow. The committee proposes financial descisions to the congregation for approval or denial.

Discipleship

Director: Chung Lee

This ministry serves our pre-existing members and helps them further grow their faith. Through Bible studies, doctrinal classes, and apologetics, it strives to create spiritually healthy servants of Christ.

Compassion/Food Pantry

Director: Evelyn Anderson

Our source of outreach, this ministry includes our food pantry. Our food pantry distribution takes place biweekly in partnership with the Houston Food Bank. The goal of this ministry is to outreach into the local community.

Youth

Director: Younhe Shin

As a Church, we recognize the importance of fostering the next generation to walk with Christ. Our youth ministry offers weekly Bible studies, youth nights and events, and spiritual mentorships.

Little Pathways

Director: Charles Pang

Little Pathways is the childrens ministry of our Church. The ministry offers children's classes adjacent with service times to accomodate for parents. The classes comprise of age-appropriate Biblical teachings and worship.

Property

Director: Chung Lee

This committee bears the heavy burden of maintenance and repair of the building including but not limited to: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, carpentry, kitchen, and more. This ministry also manages the Church's rental and building usage relationships.
