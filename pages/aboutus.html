About us

What do we Believe?

Pathways Church of Houston is denominationally a Presbyterian Church (USA) congregation. Pastors, layleaders, and members follow Presbyterian guidelines and rules. For more information on Presbyterian doctrine, visit the PCUSA website here. Our Church is committed to teaching Biblical doctrine and will never compromise any manner of the Gospel or Holy Scripture.


Confession of Faith

We believe in God omnipotent, Creator of the universe. We believe in the Trinity, God the Father, God the Son, and God the Spirit. We believe Jesus Christ came down as God incarnate and died on the cross as perfect atonement for our sins. We believe Christ rose from the grave after 3 days and is now seated at the right hand of the father. We believe that Christ will come again and judge the wicked from the righteous.

Our Congregational Family

The Church is the body of Christ. We are each other's brothers and sisters, and are called to treat each other as such. We are a multicultural, multigenerational, and socio-economically diverse congregation with the purpose of spreading the Gospel in each of our lives and the community around us.

Our Corporate Worship

Our worship takes place each Sunday morning and is made up of communion, contemporary and traditional praise, confessions, a sermon, prayer, offering, and a charge and benediction. An adjacent time for the children to enjoy age-appropriate praise and activity lessons from the Bible is offered in parallel.

Our Connection Groups

Members are called to join one of our small group ministries where we believe true evangelism can take place by reaching out to people in our personal lives. Members also take this time to reconnect with one another and God. Groups meet throughout the Houston area on various days and times.
