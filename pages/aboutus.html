What do we Believe?

Pathways Church of Houston is denominationally a Presbyterian Church (USA) congregation. Pastors, layleaders, and members follow Presbyterian guidelines and rules. For more information on Presbyterian doctrine, visit the PCUSA website here. Our Church is committed to Biblical doctrine and will never compromise any manner of the gospel.

***We are not affiliated with More Light Presbyterians nor the Covenant Network.
