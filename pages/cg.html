Connection Groups

Connection groups are small ministries for members to have personal fellowship through breaking bread and intentional conversations. These small groups not only help members grow in their faith, but are also a source of outreach into the community. Groups are roughly divided into geographical regions and meet according to their appropriate times.

×

Northwest

Led by: Greg Feigh

A diverse group spanning across Northwest Houston, we have a little bit of everyone. We love fellowship and breaking bread together. We meet weekly on weekends.

×

Make It So

Led by: Ki Shin and Soojin Jang

Inspired by the famous sci-fi show Star Trek, the name "Make it So," reinforces the idea of following God's commands, thus making his words so. We meet biweekly on Saturday evenings.

×

Coffee Talk

Led by: Tim and Sharon Hattenbauch

Coffee talk is a traditional Sunday school group, meeting each Sunday at the church at 9:40am for Bible study and fellowship.

×

Katy Talk

Led by: Chung and Celeste

This connection group strives to build each other up through love, support each other through prayer, and share our lives through fellowship. We center conversations around Christ and how He has worked in our lives. We encompass the general Katy area and meet irregularily on weekends.

×

Acts 2:46

Led by: Joy Eng and Mary Cho

Made up of many 'foodies,' we love good food. We believe the verse Acts 2:46 encompasses who our group is made of. We meet weekly and generally cover west Houston.

×

Charles and Patricia CG

Led by: Charles and Patricia

Our connection group loves to share discussion with others and spread the Gospel. With our lives centered around Christ, we always manage to find a blessing every day. We meet irregularily on weekends.
